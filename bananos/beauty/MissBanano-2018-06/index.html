General Rules

  1. All Contestants must be of the age of majority.
  2. All photos must be legal, and you agree the contest can use them.
  3. No Full Nudity for Miss Banano. Partial nudity is acceptable.
  4. No pornography.
  5. All contestants must appear to be Female. If you aren't female but can pass as female, we can't stop you but you may not win the contest
  6. If you are obviously a male dressed as a female, then you do not pass as female. Judges have final say.
  7. No professional photos, we don't want any copyright problems.
  8. Use Natural Light if possible.
  9. Use a neutral background if possible.
  10. Avoid including anything that would help someone harras you, like your street address
  11. Judges have final say in all matters

Payouts

Judges will rank all contestants that pass verification (If your image is not on here, you did not pass verification).

winners will be determined by Instant Runoff Voting.
